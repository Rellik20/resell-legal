# Community Guidelines (ReSell)

Effective date: 2026-02-13

ReSell is a local marketplace with messaging. These rules exist to keep users safe. By using ReSell, you agree to follow these Guidelines.

---

## 1) Prohibited content and items (do not post)
You may not list, request, or promote:
- Illegal items or stolen goods
- Drugs, drug paraphernalia, or controlled substances
- Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives, or weapon parts
- Pornography, nudity, sexual services, or exploitation
- Hate speech, harassment, threats, or incitement of violence
- Content involving minors in any sexual or exploitative context
- Counterfeit items or intellectual property infringement
- Tobacco/vapes, alcohol, or other restricted goods (unless ReSell explicitly supports and complies with local law)
- Personal data doxxing (addresses, SSNs, bank info, etc.)

If you are unsure whether something is allowed, do not list it.

---

## 2) Behavioral rules (do not do this)
- No scams or fraud
- No impersonation
- No harassment, intimidation, or repeated unwanted contact
- No requesting sensitive info in chat (passwords, bank details, verification codes)
- No spam, bulk messaging, or automated scraping

---

## 3) Messaging safety
- Keep communication respectful and on-topic
- Never share verification codes or financial credentials
- For in-person meetups, choose a public place and bring a friend if possible

---

## 5) Enforcement
We may take action at our discretion, including:
- removing or hiding listings
- deleting messages or conversations
- warning users
- restricting features
- suspending or disabling accounts
- cooperating with law enforcement where required

Severe violations (weapons, drugs, exploitation, threats) may result in immediate account removal.
