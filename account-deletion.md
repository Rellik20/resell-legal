# Account Deletion (ReSell)

Effective date: 2026-02-13

ReSell provides in-app account deletion.

---

## 1) How to delete your account (in the app)
1. Open the App
2. Go to **Profile**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

Deletion is intended to be permanent and cannot be undone.

---

## 2) What gets deleted
When you delete your account, ReSell deletes:
- your user account
- your listings
- listing photos (best effort removal from our image host)
- password reset records
- pending signup/verification records (if any)
- your conversations and messages in the app (best effort)

---

## 3) What other users can still see
After deletion:
- your listings should no longer be visible
- conversations/messages tied to your account are intended to be removed

---
