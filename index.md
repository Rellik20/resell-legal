# ReSell Marketplace Legal & Safety

**Effective date:** May 5, 2026  
**Operator:** Scott Cote (“ReSell Marketplace”, “ReSell”, “we”, “us”)  
**Support contact:** resell21126@gmail.com

ReSell Marketplace is a local marketplace app for adults that lets users create listings, upload item photos, search marketplace listings, search public profiles, favorite listings, message other users, and use safety tools such as reporting and blocking.

ReSell Marketplace does **not** process payments, provide escrow, guarantee item quality, verify every user, handle shipping, or mediate every transaction dispute. Users arrange transactions directly with each other at their own risk.

## Key safety and compliance points

### User-generated content

Listings, listing photos, profile information, profile photos, screenshots attached to reports, usernames, public profiles, favorites activity, and messages may involve user-generated content or user-submitted materials.

ReSell Marketplace may review and moderate content when needed to keep the platform safe, including hiding listings, restricting features, preserving moderation records, reviewing reports, limiting messaging, or disabling accounts. We aim to review and act on safety-related reports as quickly as reasonably possible, generally within 24 hours when practical.

### In-app safety tools

ReSell Marketplace provides:

- In-app reporting tools for listings and users
- In-app blocking tools
- Unblocking from the profile menu where supported
- Report categories and optional report details
- Optional screenshots/evidence images for reports
- Published support contact information
- Account deletion initiated in-app

If you see something unsafe, illegal, abusive, fraudulent, or prohibited, use the in-app reporting tools or contact us at **resell21126@gmail.com**.

### Prohibited items and behavior

ReSell Marketplace is for lawful person-to-person resale only. Do not list, request, message about, or facilitate anything illegal under federal, state, local, or other applicable law.

Prohibited categories include controlled substances, weapons, stolen goods, counterfeit items, alcohol, tobacco/nicotine products, prescription medications, explicit sexual content, threats, harassment, scams, hate, exploitation, or content involving child safety concerns.

Full details are in the [Community Guidelines](community-guidelines) and [Terms of Service](terms-of-service).

### Location privacy

To show nearby listings and approximate distance, ReSell Marketplace may store private coordinates for distance calculations. The app is designed to display only an approximate **City/State-style public location label** and approximate distance where applicable, not exact coordinates.

Users can deny location permission and use custom/manual location options where available.

### Photos and image storage

Users may upload listing photos, profile photos, or optional report evidence images. ReSell Marketplace currently stores and delivers uploaded images through Cloudflare R2. Older images may have previously used legacy providers such as Cloudinary.

Camera and photo access are used only when the user chooses to add or upload images.

### Messages and notifications

Messaging is available for marketplace communication. ReSell Marketplace may send app-related notifications, such as message notifications or service updates, if the user allows notifications.

Messages may be reviewed when reasonably necessary for safety, abuse prevention, moderation, legal compliance, or support.

### Public profiles, people search, and favorites

Version 2.0.0 includes public profiles, people search, marketplace search, listing favorites, public-profile listing sections, and improved marketplace browsing.

Users should not use these features to harass, stalk, scrape, spam, impersonate, or evade moderation.

### Official update stories

Version 2.0.0 includes an official ReSell Marketplace update story/announcement area used for app updates and release information. This is not currently a general user-created story feature.

### Features not currently offered

As of version 2.0.0, ReSell Marketplace does not currently offer:

- In-app subscriptions
- Paid marketplace events
- Payment processing
- Escrow
- Shipping
- General user-created story posting

If these features are added in a future update, these policies may be updated.

### Account identity

ReSell Marketplace may assign or store:

- A username
- A public user identifier
- An internal ReSell user/account ID

These may be used for account management, safety review, support, moderation, and platform integrity.

### Age requirement

ReSell Marketplace is intended for **adults 18+**.

### Account deletion

Users can delete their account **in-app**. When deleted, ReSell Marketplace removes, deletes, disables, or de-identifies core account content as described on the [Account Deletion](account-deletion) page.

Certain limited safety, abuse-prevention, legal, audit, and moderation records may be retained where reasonably necessary or legally required.
