# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 12, 2026  
**Operator:** Scott Cote (“ReSell”, “we”, “us”)  
**Contact:** resell21126@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how ReSell collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the ReSell mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

## 1. Summary (plain language)

ReSell collects account information, listing content, approximate public location labels, private coordinates used for nearby discovery, and user-to-user messages. ReSell also supports in-app reporting and blocking, and may collect moderation-related information such as reports, categories selected in reports, optional screenshots attached to reports, and safety/enforcement records.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Account information
- **Email address**
- **Password hash** (we do not store your plaintext password)
- Username
- Internal **ReSell User ID**
- Account metadata (created/updated timestamps)
- Account status and safety flags (for example: disabled, deleted, role)
- Terms / policy acceptance metadata

### 2.2 Listings and user-generated content
- Listing title, description, price
- Listing photos you upload
- Listing status (active/sold/hidden where applicable)
- City/State location label shown to other users
- Creation/update timestamps

### 2.3 Location information
ReSell supports “nearby” discovery. Depending on how you set location, we may collect:
- **Private precise coordinates (latitude/longitude)** used to calculate distance and show nearby listings
- **Public City/State label** displayed to other users

**Privacy design:** ReSell is designed to show other users only your **City/State label** and not your exact coordinates.

You can deny location permission and instead type a City/State manually.

### 2.4 Messaging data
- Messages you send and receive (text content)
- Message timestamps and read status
- Conversation metadata (such as last message preview and time)

### 2.5 Reports, blocking, and moderation data
If you use safety features, we may collect:
- Reports you submit
- The type of content or user being reported
- The report category you choose
- Free-text report details you provide
- Optional screenshots or other image evidence you attach to a report
- Block / unblock relationships
- Moderator actions and related timestamps
- Snapshot information needed to preserve moderation history if an account is later deleted

### 2.6 Technical and security data
- Basic device/app information necessary to operate the Service
- Request metadata, logs, and error information
- IP address and related infrastructure/security logs may be processed by infrastructure providers for security and reliability

## 3. How we use information

We use information to:
- Create and manage accounts
- Authenticate users and secure access
- Generate and maintain account identity fields such as usernames and internal ReSell User IDs
- Host and display listings
- Provide nearby discovery and distance-based feeds
- Deliver messaging functionality
- Support in-app reporting, evidence review, blocking, and safety workflows
- Prevent abuse, spam, fraud, prohibited transactions, and policy violations
- Enforce our Terms and Community Guidelines
- Maintain, debug, secure, and improve the Service
- Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

## 4. How we share information

### 4.1 With other users
When you use the Service, some information is visible to other users depending on the feature:
- Your listing content (photos, title, description, price)
- Your **City/State label**
- Messaging content with the person you message
- Your username where shown in app features

ReSell is designed **not** to share your exact listing coordinates with other users.

### 4.2 With service providers (processors)
We use third-party providers to run the Service. These providers process data on our behalf, including:
- **Cloud storage / image hosting** for listing photos and report evidence images
- **Database hosting** for app data
- **Email delivery** for verification codes and password reset codes
- **Infrastructure, security, and logging** for reliability and abuse prevention

These providers are authorized to process data only as needed to provide services to ReSell, and we expect them to protect user data in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

### 4.3 For legal and safety reasons
We may disclose information if we believe it is necessary to:
- Comply with law, regulation, legal process, or government request
- Enforce our Terms or Community Guidelines
- Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, abuse, or technical issues
- Protect the rights, property, or safety of ReSell, our users, or the public

### 4.4 Business changes
If ReSell is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

## 5. Content moderation and message review

ReSell may review listings, messages, reports, and screenshots attached to reports when reasonably necessary to:
- Investigate reports and abuse
- Enforce safety rules and prohibited content policies
- Respond to legal requests or credible safety threats
- Preserve safety and moderation history

We do not promise proactive monitoring of all content, but we reserve the right to review content and related materials when needed for safety, integrity, and compliance.

## 6. Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business, security, safety, and legal purposes.

Typical retention:
- Account data is kept until you delete your account or it is removed for policy reasons.
- Listings and messages are generally kept until deletion or enforcement action, unless needed longer for safety, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, or legal obligations.
- Reports, evidence attached to reports, and related moderation records may be retained for abuse prevention, legal compliance, audit, and platform integrity purposes even if an account is later deleted.

## 7. Account deletion

You can delete your account in the app. When you delete your account, ReSell removes, deletes, or de-identifies core account content associated with your account, such as:
- Your account’s active access to the Service
- Your listings
- Listing images (best effort)
- Password reset and pending signup records
- Conversations and messages associated with your account (best effort)

Some limited information may be retained where required by law or for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, audit, and moderation purposes, including certain reports and moderation snapshots.

## 8. Your choices and controls

### 8.1 Location permissions
- You can deny location permission and manually enter a City/State.
- You can change location settings in your device settings.

### 8.2 Safety controls
- You can report listings or users in-app
- You can block other users in-app
- You can provide optional screenshots as evidence when making a report

### 8.3 Email communications
We send emails for account verification and password reset. If we introduce marketing emails in the future, we will provide opt-out choices as required by law.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 10. Children’s privacy

ReSell is intended for adults. Users must be **18+**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 11. International use

ReSell is operated from the United States. If you use the Service from outside the U.S., you understand your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. and other countries where service providers operate.

## 12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
